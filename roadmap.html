INTRODUCTION: THE SUPREME CAUSE

The title may seem like it’s suggesting something pretentious, which is correct. The utmost objective of this NFT collection, is to combine the real life or metaverse with NFT’s via some social events.

It cannot be ignored that the crypto currencies have altered most of the procedures of the global finance systems, since they have provided much more secure foundation. It is a matter of time to observe the change in the asset industry as well, since NFT’s will also provide a more secure, fast, and clear foundation. The fastest changes may be with assets that people buy for the entertainment and travel industry since they can be implemented easily due to the fact that these tickets can be consumed in a small period of time, and this would increase the trust to the concept. On the other hand, these changes may happen in a blink of a time, without all these processes, if a global trading brand decides to implement it to their system. Releasing an NFT collection with this idea on our minds, is clearly serving this cause. One of the most exciting parts of contributing to an NFT project is being able become an investor without having accumulated a serious amount of capital. In real life, it is almost impossible to be an investor in a company with the transparency that the NFT projects are offering to everyone. MetaLions NFT is a collection of 5000 detailed and custom-designed Lions that are willing to contribute for this goal.

BUILDING THE COMMUNITY:

It is an undeniable fact that the most important pillar of an NFT project is the community. It should not be overlooked that an NFT project has many important pillars that need to be brainstormed, and in some scenarios, even the absence of one leg can cause the project to fail.

Building a community requires special attention due to many aspects. It is also obvious that community should have its reasons to contribute to the project. For NFT projects, these reasons are mostly financial, and the community is mainly seeking items that will benefit them financially. Although the project provides very valid reasons for the community to support the project, the most important element is that the community supports itself and therefore the project.

We are planning to create another group within the community which is a private group named “The Lion’s Share Club”, with some requirements to join, and basically, the indicator of being a part of this community will be acquiring a Silver or Gold VIP Card. These requirements are mainly to assess the value of being a member of this club. Specifically, the requirement of getting a Silver VIP Card is by exchanging it with 3 MetaLions, and similarly, to acquire a Gold VIP Card is to burn 5 MetaLions.

ASSESSING THE VALUE:

Obviously an NFT must have a value in order to make it worth buying, otherwise it would become a worthless asset. One of the most important contents an NFT has will undoubtedly be its market value, which gets us into a vicious circle. Because all the content created for NFTs also affects the market value, whereas scenarios in which the market value also affects itself have been seen.

While an ordinary NFT project has only one asset to value, we have three; MetaLions NFT, Silver VIP Card and Gold VIP Card. It may seem hard to achieve this but as mentioned above, the strategy applied will be extraordinary. In order to distribute 500 Silver VIP Cards, there will be 1500 MetaLions burnt and likewise, there will be 500 MetaLions burnt for 100 Gold VIP Cards which will drastically increase the value of the MetaLions.

As can be seen in our roadmap, social events in both the metaverse and the real life will be organized. Furthermore, it’s been planned to create an exclusive group for such events. Other than that, MetaLion Land and $LION airdrop will take place for the holders of the VIP Cards. Last but not least, MetaLion Land will be introduced as a demo before the launch to the community

Silver VIP Card owners will be able to attend special social events and receive MetaLion Land gifts. Gold VIP Card owners will have all the rights that Silver VIP Card has, plus receive $LION and $SOL drops along with some other special airdrops. It’s beneficial to state that $LION token supply will be 2,000,000.

OUR VISION:

As mentioned in the introduction paragraph, our ultimate goal will be serving the real cause of that will definitely impact all of the projects if succeeded. Because this will once again prove the world the activity that NFTs may offer.

Furthermore, we will strengthen our hand by developing a game and implementing it with our token for a P2E system. We will set the project up as much transparent as possible where there won’t be any bosses, instead the project will be serving itself, while offering the best of its support to the people who are contributing to the community. So one of the things that we aim in the long-term is to provide the financial foundation to our community so that they can make a living out of the above-mentioned goals.

We would love to see you with us on this journey!
